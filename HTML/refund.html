Refund & Returns Policy

Last Updated: June 2023

We want you to love your Glamour Cosmetics purchase! Please review our policy carefully before initiating a return.

1 Eligibility for Returns

  • Unopened Products: Full refund within 30 days with original packaging and receipt
  • Damaged/Defective Items: Must report within 7 days with photo/video evidence
  • Wrong Items Received: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery

2 Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used makeup products (for hygiene reasons)
  • Customized products (personalized compacts, custom shade mixes)
  • Sale/clearance items (unless defective)
  • Gift cards and downloadable products

3 Return Process

  1. Request Authorization: Email returns@glamourcosmetics.com with your order number
  2. Package Securely: Include all original packaging and free gifts
  3. Ship Back: Send to our returns center within 14 days of approval
Return Type Who Pays Shipping? Processing Time
Change of mind Customer 7-10 business days
Wrong item sent Glamour Cosmetics 3-5 business days
Damaged product Glamour Cosmetics 5-7 business days

4 Refund Options

  • Original Payment: Refunded within 5-10 business days after return processing
  • Store Credit: Issued immediately (includes 10% bonus for future purchases)
  • Replacements: Available for defective/damaged items (subject to stock)

5 Special Cases

  • Allergic Reactions: Requires medical certificate for refund consideration
  • International Returns: Customer responsible for customs/duties
  • Subscription Boxes: Must cancel before next billing cycle

Important: We reserve the right to refuse returns that don't meet our policy requirements. All approved refunds will be processed within 10 business days after we receive your return.
